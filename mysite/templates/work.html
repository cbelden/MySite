MTS Systems Corporation

Summer 2013

This past summer I interned as a software test engineer at MTS Systems in Eden Prairie, MN. MTS produces test devices for a variety of fields including the materials, automotive, and aerospace industries. Many of the test frames come equipped with a rich Windows application, TestSuite.

I was part of the automated test team at MTS, and I spent the summer implementing UI drivers and writing automated UI test cases (in C#) for TestSuite. It was rewarding converting time intensive manual tests into automated processes that could be run every night (versus crammed in at the end of every release!).

Gentex Corporation

Summer 2012

During the summer of 2012 I interned as a software engineer at Gentex in Zeeland, Michigan. Gentex manufactures car mirrors, and I primarily worked on the firmware (written in C) developing commands to exercise the mirror's functionality (controlling automatic dimming, rear view displays, GPS, compass, etc.). At the end of the summer, I wrote integration tests in Ruby which exercised various components of the firmware.
